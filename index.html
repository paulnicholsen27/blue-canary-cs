About Us

Blue Canary Web Design

Blue Canary Web Design partners with small businesses and organizations across a wide range of industries. Whether you run a local shop, a professional service, or a mission-driven organization, our focus is the same: clear, honest, well-crafted websites that help you grow.

We also offer discounted pricing for organizations whose work aligns with values of democracy, equality, and human kindness. This includes groups focused on community impact, inclusion, civic engagement, or social good.

Our services include custom website design, redesigns of outdated sites, performance and accessibility improvements, mobile-first layouts, and ongoing support. We take a practical, collaborative approach and aim to build sites that are easy to maintain, fast to load, and effective for real people.

Our goal is to be thoughtful about the work we take on, while remaining open, practical, and easy to work with.
